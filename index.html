About Us

Welcome to Delhi Zafran Family Restaurant, your premier destination for authentic Indian cuisine in Thane, Maharashtra. Our Castle Mill Branch brings you the warmth of traditional family dining combined with modern comfort.

🏡 Family-Friendly Dining

We believe that the best meals are shared with loved ones. Our restaurant is designed to provide a comfortable, welcoming atmosphere where families can gather, celebrate, and create lasting memories. Whether it's a birthday celebration, a family reunion, or simply a weekend dinner, we ensure every visit is special.

🌶️ Authentic Indian Flavours

Each dish is prepared with passion and precision, using traditional recipes and premium ingredients. Our master chefs craft every meal to perfection, bringing you the true taste of India's diverse culinary heritage. From mild to spicy, from vegetarian to non-vegetarian options, there's something for everyone.

✨ Comfortable Atmosphere

Step into our elegantly designed restaurant and experience tranquility and warmth. Our soft lighting, premium furnishings, and attention to detail create an ambiance that's both sophisticated and inviting. It's the perfect blend of premium and comfort.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Perfect for Every Occasion

From intimate family dinners to large gatherings, our spacious dining areas can accommodate all your needs. Our attentive staff is dedicated to providing exceptional service, ensuring your experience exceeds expectations.
